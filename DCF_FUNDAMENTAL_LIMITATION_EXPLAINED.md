### 1. DCF is a Conservative Intrinsic Value Model
DCF discounts future cash flows to present value using:
- A reasonable discount rate (8-12% WACC)
- Sustainable growth rates (2-4% terminal growth)
- Finite projection period (5-10 years)

**This methodology is designed for mature companies**, not platforms trading at 1,000+ EBITDA multiples.

### 2. What Would It Take to Get $469B in DCF?

To value Palantir at $469B using DCF, you would need:

**Option A - Absurd Growth Rates:**
- Terminal growth rate: 25-30% (IMPOSSIBLE - no company grows 25% forever)
- This violates Gordon Growth Model (terminal growth must be < WACC)

**Option B - Negative Discount Rate:**
- WACC: -5% to 0% (IMPOSSIBLE - negative time value of money)

**Option C - Infinite Cash Flows:**
- Assume Palantir generates $50B+ EBITDA in 10 years (100x current)
- This is speculative, not valuation

**All options break the DCF model mathematically.**
